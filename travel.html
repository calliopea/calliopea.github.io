    This Mountain

    This mountain, located in the middle of the ocean is a quiet and calm spot to read a book.
    If you climb all the way to the top, you can find the Grinch. The Grinch is a green, mean
    creature who hates Christmas. Besides the Grinch, you will have a nice time on the
    mountain.

    Activities

    • Climb to the top
    • Read a book
    • Hangout with the Grinch

    What to Eat

    Some yucky brussel sprouts, the Grinch's favorite food.

    This Field

    This field is a nice escape for anone who's feling the pressures of the world. You can enjoy
    a nice run through the golden grain and maybe find some hidden treasure! Native animals
    inclued grain bugs, grain cows and birds. If you're lucky, you'll come when it is grain
    harvesting season and you can harvest grain to make fresh baked bread!

    Activities

    • Run through the field
    • Look for treasure
    • Pick grain and make bread

    What to Eat

    A yummy croissant that you made with your harvested wheat.
