    First post!

    2/4/2008

    Wow! I starteted a blog! No one said I would be able to do it! They told
    me that I would never keep it updated. They said I would do 1 or 2 posts
    before I got bored and would stop. Well look at me now! I have a blog!

    "What's my blog going to be about?," you ask. It's going to be about
    everything. I'll tell you all bout my greatest fears (snakes), my dreams,
    my favorite things and all the things I wonder about. For example, what
    would happen if a skeleton learned how to dance? Would is be delightful or terrifying?
